About Jainico

With more than 55 years of experience, JAINICO is a major player in Metal Pretreatment & Specialty Chemicals. Our organization is professionally managed and fully equipped with testing and quality control laboratories to ensure consistent quality and optimum performance. We manufacture a diverse range of Pre-treatment Chemicals for iron, steel, zinc, and aluminum, ensuring excellent paint bonding and corrosion resistance.
